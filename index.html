About This Book

This is a historical documentation written for the understanding and education of South Africa's youth and citizens. It captures the pivotal moments of 2026 as our nation addresses immigration enforcement and the protection of its sovereignty.

The author believes in the power of historical documentation to educate future generations about the challenges and triumphs of their nation.
