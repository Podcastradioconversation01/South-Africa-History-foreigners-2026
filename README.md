# South Africa History: Foreigners 2026

A historical documentation of South Africa's journey in 2026 - a pivotal year in our nation's ongoing struggle for sovereignty and freedom.

## 📖 About This Project

This book chronicles the significant events of 2026 as South Africa addresses the challenge of illegal immigration and enforces its laws to protect the rights and freedoms of its citizens. This project is dedicated to educating the youth and citizens of South Africa about this important historical period.
